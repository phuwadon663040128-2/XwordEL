FAQ

What is XwordEL?

XwordEL, also known as the XWORDEL-PROJECT, is a web application for crossword puzzles that helps with learning English vocabulary while enjoying the game.

What does XwordEL stand for?

XwordEL stands for "Crossword for English Learning."

How can I create my own crossword puzzle?

You can create your own crossword puzzle by uploading PDF or TXT files.

What's the difference between File mode and Normal mode?

In File mode, you can create your own crossword puzzles. In Normal mode, we create the crossword puzzle for you.

How do clue types work?

Choosing "Thai" will provide clues in Thai, and selecting "English" will give you clues in English.

What does "number of clue types" mean?

It refers to the different meanings words can have.

How does difficulty work?

"Each letter" mode checks the answer letter by letter, and "whole word" mode requires the entire word to be correct.

How does the vowel letter hint work?

The vowel letter hint shows the vowels (a, e, i, o, u) in the crossword puzzle.

What is the bar at the top of the game?

It represents the percentage of the puzzle that has been solved.

What do the asterisks (*) in the clue section mean?

Asterisks indicate that an exact match for a word couldn't be found, so we show the closest match. For example, if "take" is not found but "taken" is, it will show as ****n.

Why is the percentage bar not 0% at the start or 100% at the end sometimes?

Due to the API we use, if a word is not found, it is counted as a played word, which can affect the starting or ending percentage of the bar.

Any tips?

You can click on a clue to automatically highlight the corresponding part of the puzzle.
